Saint Louis Gateway Arch

Gateway Arch is a 630-foot-tall (192 m) monument in St. Louis, Missouri, united States. Clad in stainless stell and built in the form of a weighted cantenary arch, it is the world's tallest arch and Missouri's tallest accessible structure. Some sources consider it the tallest human-made monument in the Western Hemisphere.
